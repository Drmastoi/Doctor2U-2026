# Medical Blog Editorial Calendar & Strategy (2026)

## Core Objective
Establish Doctor2U as a thought leader in private GP care and preventive functional medicine in the North West (Lancashire & Manchester).

## Target Audience
- Busy professionals in Manchester seeking fast medical access.
- Families in Lancashire looking for unhurried clinical care.
- Seniors interested in longevity and chronic disease management.
- Professional drivers requiring medical compliance.

## Content Pillars
1. **Clinical Excellence:** Deep dives into symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment plans.
2. **Preventative Health:** Longevity, MOT screenings, and lifestyle medicine.
3. **Local Community:** Health trends and services specific to Manchester/Lancashire.
4. **Technology in Health:** How AI and digital tools enhance (but don't replace) the human doctor.

## SEO Guidelines
- Every post must link to at least one relevant service page (e.g., MOT article links to `service-health-screening`).
- Include the "Doctor2U" brand message: "Private Doctor Care Enhanced by AI".
- Use semantic headers (H2, H3) and bullet points for readability.
- All images must include descriptive Alt Text for accessibility.

## Internal Linking Plan
- **Service Pages -> Blog:** Link from "Chronic Disease Management" to the Diabetes and Stress articles.
- **Blog -> Service Pages:** Link from the MOT article to the "Book Online" primary CTA.
- **Hero Section -> Blog:** Highlight the latest article on the Homepage.
